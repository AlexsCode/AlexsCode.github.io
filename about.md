# About Me

Solution Architect in CSL's IoT Engineering Solutions team. I architect, design, and develop eSIM orchestration systems — including our flagship eUICC product, **rSIM**.

Specialist in **SGP.32 eSIM** and active member of the **eSIM work group**, collaborating with industry experts to push GSMA standards forward. My work spans the full stack: from low-level modem integration and cellular protocol deep-dives to orchestration platforms and secure element architecture.

Previously led firmware development on RTOS-based alarm communicators, built autonomous HIL test harnesses, and served as Scrum Master. Always chasing the next hard problem.
