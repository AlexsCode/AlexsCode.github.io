# About Me

Embedded software engineer with a broad technical skillset spanning firmware, hardware, testing, and CI/CD. I build things that live at the intersection of software and physical hardware — from bare-metal MCU firmware to fully autonomous test harnesses.

Currently at **CSL-Group** as a Software Developer in Devices & Firmware, where I develop RTOS-based alarm communicators, design secure-boot solutions, build CI/CD pipelines, and serve as the team's modem and cellular expert. Also the team's Scrum Master for 2 years — because good process makes good code.

I'm always chasing the next hard problem and the next technique to master. TDD, Rust, LLMs, cellular protocol deep-dives — if it's challenging and under-documented, I'm interested.
